Introduction

Jusda Supply Chain is more alive than ever. By unifying all the supplier workspace with chain service will bring each of them much more efficient.
With the much easier interface and workflow, all information at a glance. simple clicks to finish the daily task. No more finding important information in the sea of numbers.

Design Core

Simple, Modern, Humanized

The intention of the simple design is that it will be easy to change, being adaptable to the changing needs, without making everything configurable. Rather than planning for points in the future where the application will likely change, we can build software that can easily change At any point, by applying simple design principles.

Design Principles

1. The website designed by common computer 21-inch screen with resolution 1440x900 as bast resolution of viewing.
2. The website color tone base on Jusda global system and added extra colors as an embellishment.
3. The dark version color tone base on dark blue as perfect combination with Jusda global yellow instead total black.
4. The number information blocks are flexible in 2, 3 and 4 blocks per row, use depends on suppliers needs.
5. The linear graph can use for 2 per row, depends on the amount of information.

Designer Selection

Jusda Supply chain is huge, full of information. People have to go through the numbers all the time and that makes them exhausted. They need some softer interface to balance and simple workflow. The Idea of designer selection was made for it. With a clean, simple interface and round shape of button and blocks. With some extra warm colors for embellishment, make people who work under a bunch of documents a nicer break.
